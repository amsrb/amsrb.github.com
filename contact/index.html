Contact Us

Get In Touch

Want to talk to one of the organizers? Contact us at contact@rubynl.org with your question or sponsorship request.

Interested in learning more about Stichting Ruby NL, the organization behind Amsterdam.rb and similar events? Visit the rubynl.org website.

Give A Talk

Interested in giving a talk at a future meetup? Contact us at contact@rubynl.org with a talk title and a short description. Talks are usually between 20 and 30 minutes, plus some extra time for Questions and Answers. While we're doing remote meetups, it's also possible to send in your talk prerecorded.

We can also be reached on Twitter @amsrb (DMs are open), or on the community Slack (our handles are: @Arno - he/him, @FloorD, @tombruijn and @rayta).

Join The Community

Interested in meeting new people and talk Ruby and everything else? Join our Slack and introduce yourself!
