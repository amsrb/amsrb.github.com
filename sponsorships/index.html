Becoming a Sponsor

Sponsorships

Sponsoring the Amsterdam.rb meetup helps out the community organize monthly events where we come together to learn new things and meet each other. If you're looking to sponsor a meetup event, please read for more details.

Contact us if you want to host a meetup or have any questions.

All sponsors are required to read and adhere to our Code of Conduct. We ask all sponsors to read and agree with our sponsorship agreement.

Sponsorship details

Hosting an Amsterdam.rb means your location is accessible, and you have the following on-site to facilitate the meetup:

  1. A projector, beamer or a large monitor and connectors therefor.
  2. Plenty of seating. It's possible to set up attendee limits.
  3. Enough beverages for the attendees, including non-alcoholic.
  4. Food. Pizza is an easy choice, but feel free to mix it up. Consider including some vegetarian and vegan options.

In return, we'll provide:

  • A mention, logo and link on amsrb.org website, like the ones below this section.
  • A mention on the meetup.com event page.
  • A logo and mention in the meetup's slides, with an opportunity to give a short introduction in person.
  • An @-mention on social media.
  • As a sponsor you're always welcome to give away goodies, such as T-shirts, stickers, mugs, socks, etc.
