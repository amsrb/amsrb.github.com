Next meetup

More than Ruby

At Codaisseur Academy

HElLo RuBy-explorers 👩🏾‍🚀🚀,

This edition we will explore the world beyond Ruby! Let's talk about Resilience and Accessibility...

We are doing this live at Codaisseur, in Amsterdam. Codaisseur will not just host our event, they will also provide us with food 🥦 and drinks 🧃.

It goes without saying that we have a great set of speakers again. Our first guest is the marvelous, Bart de Water on how to build resilient systems.

Our second guest is the astonishing Derk-Jan Karrenbeld, CTO at NoticeSound, Co-Founder at Delft Solutions with a talk on accessibility.

This location is completely (wheelchair) accessible. Don't feel like joining in person? We will live stream the meetup on YouTube, and it will stay up after the event.
