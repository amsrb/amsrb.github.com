Dear Ruby friends 🐱🐶🐰🐯,

It's been a while since the last Amsterdam.rb event 👋 We hope everyone is still doing well during this time. Our next meetup will be another virtual online event. Please RSVP so we have an idea of the audience size we can expect.

The livestream can be found on YouTube channel at the time of event. Subscribe to the RubyNL channel. We will send out a messages with the YouTube livestream link before the event.

All attendees can talk and ask questions with the community and speakers on the Ruby Netherlands Slack. Join our Slack today. We will be chatting in the #amsterdam channel during this event, so don't forget to join the #amsterdam channel.

With this being a virtual meetup, food and beverages will be of the "bring your own" kind. Grab your most comfy chair and your favorite snacks and join us online!

See you on the interwebs!
Arno, Floor, Tom & Rayta
