Next Meet-Up online event

Well heck, Ruby friends,

We miss conferencing with you all! That's why August 21 we'll host #NoRuKo, an online mini-conference! The name is a play on the biggest European Ruby Conference, "EuRuKo", which we organized last year, in Rotterdam. It's been quiet in the Ruby world in terms of online events, with a few (great!!) exceptions, but no more!

Join for ~12 talks, quizzes, virtual #RubyKaraoke / #Quaraoke, and more: noruko.org

With this being a virtual event, food and beverages will be of the "bring your own" kind. Grab a comfy chair and your favorite snacks and join us online!

See you on the interwebs!
