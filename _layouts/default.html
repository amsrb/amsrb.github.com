Our meet-up point

80beans

June 19, 2012

Vijzelstraat 72 - 2.38, Amsterdam at 19:00

After being dormant for a while the Amsterdam Ruby User Group is back! On June 19 we will kick-off with "Amsterdam.rb Reboot" at the 80beans office, which will hopefully be the first of a long line of regular events.

Josh Kalderimis will introduce the current team running Ams.rb, what we have planned for the near future and how you can contribute. Right after we have three lightning talks lined up.

Of course there's plenty of time for socializing and 80beans will be providing us with drinks and snacks. We can handle about 80 people, so make sure to sign up!
